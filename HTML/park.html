Description

Anime Park is a great place to go for those who want to get out and enjoy nature. Though outdoor activity was limited during its opening, it still managed to win an award for one of the best parks in 2020.

If you ever get the chance to come out and visit, don't forget to participate in several of our activities. Spend a day fishing at the lake with Luffy, play chess at one of our many gazebos with Shikamaru, or get served nutritious meals by sous chef Sanji.

Our Staff

Vegeta

Vegeta is a legend. He will get you in shape faster than you ever could. After losing his first fight to archrival and friend Goku, Vegeta has trained relentlessly to gain back his pride as a Saiyan prince. His favorite activities include weight lifting and running. If you're thinking: "I need to get back into a healthy and active lifestyle", contact Vegeta and let him help you. His training regimen may be intense, but every client of his loves the results they have gotten.

Ichigo Kurosaki

Ichigo is the go-to-guy for getting your mind right. Born with the ability to see spiritual beings, he has encountered far more strange encounters in this world than many. When he's not working from home as a translator, he is a consultant for Animeland Gym. Today's fast paced world often finds individuals not able to balance the stresses of work, family, and home. Ichigo is an expert when it comes to dealing with some of the most intense psychological issues out there. Contact Mr. Kurosaki today.

Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke has an excellent dietary regimen. You can constantly find him munching on rice balls, peanuts, and salads prior to the start of his shifts at Animeland. Great meals often lead to great workouts. If you're looking to improve your overall quality of life, especially in the culinary aspect, Mr. Uchiha would be the perfect coach for you.
